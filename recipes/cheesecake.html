Cheesecake Recipe

Description

Cheesecake has always been a stable around the world and a family favorite for a long time,

and for good reason too ! Cheesecake is widely popular for its delicious flavor and smooth texture.

This cheesecake recipe goes out to one of my closest friends, Natanael.

Ingredients for Graham Cracker Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon brown sugar
  • 7 Tablespoons butter melted

Ingredients for Cheesecake

  • 32 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cups sour cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325F

  2. Prepare Graham Cracker crust first by combinging graham cracker crumbs,

    sugar, and brown sugar, and stirring well. Add melted butter and use

    a fork to combine ingredients well

  3. Pour crumbs into a 9" Springform pan and press firmly into the bottom

Directions for Cheesecake

  1. In a bowl add cream cheese and stir until smooth and creamy
  2. Add sugar and stir again until creamy
  3. Add sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt, and stir until well combined
  4. With mixer, slowly add lightly beaten eggs, one at a time
  5. Pour cheesecake batter into prepared springform pan
  6. Transfer to the center rack of your oven and back on 325F for 70 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and allow to cool on top of the oven for 10 minutes
  8. Allow cheesecake to cool another 1-2 hours or until near room temperature
  9. Let cool overnight or for at least 6 hours
